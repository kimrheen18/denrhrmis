1. Click the Update from the left navigation bar next the Process Payroll to show the Process Payroll window screen.

2. To procced 4 Step of Payroll Process specify what Employee, Process, Month, Year, and Period from the drop-down lists and click the Save and continue.

Process Payroll Page

3. For the step 2 Click Compute button to compute the benefits/salary of permanent employees.

Process Payroll Page

Note: A permanent employee is qualified to a Hazard Pay (which is computed as Salary times the Hazard Pay Factor of employee) provided that he/she has been present for at least 1 day, a Laundry Pay of 500.00 provided that he/she has been present for at least 10 days and a Subsistence Allowance of 150.00 for every day present.
Note: After manual editing, Hazard Pay, Laundry Pay, Subsistence Allowance and Salary of permanent employees is computed.

4. Click the Back icon to back to Process Payroll.

5. To process payroll, place a tick mark on the corresponding checkboxes that will be included in the processing of payroll.

6. When done, click the Compute button to start processing of payroll. A confirmation message will be displayed if the action was successful.

7. Click the Process History from the left navigation bar to view the Process History window.

Process Payroll Page

Note: The Cashier Assistant can only view the Process History of records he/she processed. A Cashier Assistant is assigned through the HR Module.

8. Specify the Month and Year from the drop-down lists.

9. When done, click Search button to view processing history records that matched the parameters chosen.
